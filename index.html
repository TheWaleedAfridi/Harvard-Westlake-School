Harvard-Westlake School

Harvard-Westlake School is a prestigious, co-educational independent school located in Los Angeles, California. Established through the merger of Harvard School for Boys and Westlake School for Girls in 1991, the school has a rich history dating back to the early 20th century.

Harvard-Westlake is dedicated to providing an exceptional and rigorous college preparatory education to students in grades 7 through 12. With a commitment to academic excellence, the school fosters a dynamic learning environment that challenges students to think critically, collaborate effectively, and engage in a broad range of intellectual pursuits.

Beyond academics, Harvard-Westlake emphasizes the development of well-rounded individuals. The school offers a wide array of extracurricular activities, including competitive sports, performing arts, and community service opportunities. The goal is to nurture students' talents and interests, encouraging them to explore their passions both inside and outside the classroom.

Harvard-Westlake is known for its dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and a supportive community that values diversity, equity, and inclusion. The school's mission is to inspire students to become lifelong learners, responsible leaders, and compassionate citizens prepared to make a positive impact on the world.

   

Academic Programs at Harvard-Westlake

Harvard-Westlake School offers a comprehensive and rigorous academic curriculum designed to challenge and inspire students at every grade level. The school's commitment to excellence is reflected in a diverse range of academic programs that foster intellectual curiosity and critical thinking.

College Preparatory Education

Our college preparatory program prepares students for success in higher education and beyond. Emphasizing a strong foundation in core subjects such as English, mathematics, science, and social studies, students engage in a challenging and well-rounded curriculum that instills a love for learning.

Advanced Placement (AP) Courses

For students seeking additional academic challenges, Harvard-Westlake offers a selection of Advanced Placement courses. These courses provide an opportunity for in-depth study and the chance to earn college credit, showcasing our commitment to academic excellence and preparing students for the demands of top-tier universities.

Enrichment and Elective Programs

Beyond the core curriculum, students have the opportunity to explore their interests through a variety of enrichment and elective programs. From the arts to technology, language studies to entrepreneurship, Harvard-Westlake encourages students to pursue their passions and develop a well-rounded skill set.

Experiential Learning and Internships

Harvard-Westlake believes in the value of experiential learning. Students can participate in internships and hands-on experiences that connect classroom learning to real-world applications. These opportunities provide valuable insights, practical skills, and a deeper understanding of various fields.

By fostering a culture of academic excellence and innovation, Harvard-Westlake School ensures that students are well-prepared for the challenges of higher education and beyond, empowering them to become lifelong learners and leaders in their chosen fields.

   

Admissions at Harvard-Westlake

Harvard-Westlake School welcomes students who are eager to engage in a challenging and enriching educational experience. Our admissions process is designed to identify individuals who not only demonstrate academic excellence but also embody the values of curiosity, leadership, and a commitment to making a positive impact.

Application Process

Prospective students and their families are encouraged to explore our school community and familiarize themselves with our academic programs and extracurricular offerings. The application process typically includes the submission of academic records, teacher recommendations, and a personal statement that allows students to showcase their interests and aspirations.

Campus Tours and Open Houses

We invite interested families to visit our campus, attend open houses, and take part in guided tours to experience firsthand the vibrant learning environment at Harvard-Westlake. These opportunities provide a glimpse into our classrooms, facilities, and the supportive community that defines our school.

Interviews

As part of the admissions process, applicants may have the opportunity to participate in interviews. These conversations allow us to get to know students on a personal level, understanding their goals, passions, and how they will contribute to the Harvard-Westlake community.

Financial Aid

Harvard-Westlake is committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive student body. We offer a need-based financial aid program to assist families who may require support. Our goal is to ensure that talented and motivated students have access to the exceptional educational opportunities available at our school.

Key Dates and Deadlines

For information on application deadlines, important dates, and admissions events, please refer to our admissions calendar. We encourage prospective families to plan ahead and submit their applications in a timely manner.

At Harvard-Westlake, we believe in nurturing the next generation of leaders and thinkers. Our admissions team looks forward to getting to know prospective students and guiding them through the process of joining our community.
